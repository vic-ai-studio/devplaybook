## 5. Pre-Launch Checklist

### 2 Weeks Before
- [ ] Finalize Product Hunt assets (thumbnail, featured image, screenshots)
- [ ] Prepare maker profile at producthunt.com/@yourname
- [ ] Set up PH account with bio, photo, links
- [ ] Prepare email list notification (send on launch day)
- [ ] Prepare Twitter/LinkedIn post drafts

### 1 Week Before
- [ ] Announce upcoming launch to existing users (email/newsletter)
- [ ] Post in relevant communities (Indie Hackers, Hacker News, relevant subreddits)
- [ ] Reach out to 5-10 potential early reviewers personally
- [ ] Prepare hunter brief: what to test, what to screenshot

### Launch Day
- [ ] Submit at 5:45 AM PT (give 15 min buffer before peak)
- [ ] Post first comment on your launch (pin it) — thank hunters, ask for feedback
- [ ] Respond to every comment for first 2 hours
- [ ] Share in all channels immediately after submitting
- [ ] DM 10-20 people with PH accounts to upvote

### Post-Launch
- [ ] Track results: votes, comments, traffic, new signups
- [ ] Follow up on feature requests in comments
- [ ] Write retrospective (post on PH, Indie Hackers, Twitter)
