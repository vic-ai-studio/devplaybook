## How to Use

1. **Study by category** — Pick a section based on the role you're interviewing for.
2. **Practice answering out loud** — Don't just read answers; practice explaining them.
3. **Focus on concepts** — Interviewers want to see how you think, not just the final answer.
4. **Use the follow-up questions** — Most questions include follow-ups to deepen your understanding.

## About This Pack

- **210 total questions** across 4 categories
- Every question includes a **model answer** and **key points** to cover
- Most questions include **follow-up questions** interviewers typically ask next
- Questions are tagged by **difficulty**: `[Junior]` `[Mid]` `[Senior]`
