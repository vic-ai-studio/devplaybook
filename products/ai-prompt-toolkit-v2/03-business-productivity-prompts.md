## BP-2: SWOT Analysis

Create a SWOT analysis for {{COMPANY_OR_PRODUCT_OR_DECISION}}.

Context: {{DESCRIBE_SITUATION}}. Goal of this analysis: {{WHAT_DECISION_YOU_ARE_MAKING}}

For each quadrant (Strengths/Weaknesses/Opportunities/Threats): list 5-7 specific, concrete items. Avoid generic or obvious points.

After the SWOT, provide: top 3 strategic recommendations based on the analysis.

## BP-6: Performance Review

Write a performance review for {{EMPLOYEE_NAME}}.

Role: {{JOB_TITLE}}. Review period: {{PERIOD}}. Their work: {{DESCRIBE_ACCOMPLISHMENTS_AND_AREAS_FOR_GROWTH}}

## BP-8: OKR Creation

Context: company goal is {{COMPANY_GOAL}}. This team's area: {{RESPONSIBILITY_AREA}}. Current challenges: {{DESCRIBE}}
