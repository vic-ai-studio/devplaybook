## Key Features

### 1. Workflows (Automation Engine)

Copy.ai's most powerful feature. Workflows are visual, no-code pipelines that chain AI actions with external data sources:

- Input sources: CSV upload, HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Sheets, webhooks
- AI steps: generate copy, summarize, translate, classify
- Output destinations: Slack, email, Google Docs, webhooks

Use case example: A B2B SaaS team uses Workflows to auto-generate personalized LinkedIn outreach for 500 prospects daily, saving 10+ hours per week.

### 2. 90+ Templates

More templates than Jasper, covering:
- Marketing copy (ads, emails, landing pages)
- Sales assets (cold emails, LinkedIn DMs, objection handlers)
- Social media (captions, thread generators, hooks)
- Product copy (descriptions, FAQs, comparison pages)
- Blog content (outlines, intros, meta descriptions)

### 3. Brand Voice

Set up multiple brand voices for different products or clients. Copy.ai analyzes your existing content and learns tone, vocabulary, and formatting preferences.

### 4. Infobase (Knowledge Base)

Store company facts, product details, and brand guidelines that Copy.ai automatically references when generating content — reducing hallucinations and improving accuracy.

### 5. Team Collaboration

Shared workspaces, templates, and brand voices across unlimited team members (on paid plans).
