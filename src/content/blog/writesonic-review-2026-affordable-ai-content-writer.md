## Key Features

### 1. AI Article Writer 6.0

Writesonic's flagship content generator. Input a topic, target keyword, and competitors — it generates a full SEO-optimized article with:

- AI-generated outline
- Introduction and conclusion
- H2/H3 headers structured for featured snippets
- Automatic internal linking suggestions

**What makes it different:** The SEO optimization is baked in, not an add-on. You get a real-time SEO score as you write.

### 2. Chatsonic (Web-Enabled AI Chat)

Unlike Jasper Chat, Chatsonic can search the web. This means you can ask:

- "What are the latest AI writing tool releases in March 2026?"
- "Summarize this URL and write a blog post about it"
- "Find statistics about content marketing ROI and write an article"

This is a massive advantage for content that requires current data.

### 3. Factual Content Mode

Writesonic's "Factual" mode pulls real-time data from Google before generating content — reducing hallucinations on fact-heavy topics like product comparisons, news summaries, and technical guides.

### 4. 80+ Templates

Covers all major use cases:
- Blog posts (intros, outlines, full articles)
- Landing pages (hero sections, feature bullets, CTAs)
- Ads (Google, Meta, LinkedIn)
- Email (subject lines, body, follow-ups)
- Product descriptions (Shopify, Amazon, eBay)
- Social media (captions, hashtags, thread hooks)

### 5. Brand Voice (Limited)

Writesonic has brand voice features but they're less sophisticated than Jasper's. Works for basic tone consistency; not ideal for strict multi-brand agencies.
